Welcome to Delhi

Location

Delhi, the capital city of India, is situated in the northern part of the country. It is bordered by the states of Haryana and Uttar Pradesh.

About

Historical Significance

Delhi has a rich history that spans over several centuries. It has been the capital of various empires and dynasties, each leaving its mark on the city's architecture, culture, and traditions.

Cultural Melting Pot

Delhi is known for its diverse and vibrant culture. People from different parts of India and around the world have made Delhi their home, resulting in a fusion of languages, cuisines, and traditions.

Modern Infrastructure

Alongside its rich history, Delhi is also a modern metropolis with bustling markets, shopping malls, and impressive infrastructure. It is home to numerous educational institutions, government offices, and multinational corporations.

Things To Do

  • Explore the historical landmarks like the Red Fort, Jama Masjid, and Humayun's Tomb
  • Take a rickshaw ride through the bustling streets of Old Delhi
  • Visit the iconic India Gate and enjoy a leisurely stroll in the surrounding gardens
  • Experience the spiritual tranquility at Akshardham Temple
  • Shop for traditional handicrafts and textiles at Chandni Chowk
  • Indulge in the diverse street food of Delhi, including chaat, kebabs, and parathas
  • Explore the vibrant art galleries and museums, such as the National Museum and National Gallery of Modern Art
  • Take a walk through the lush gardens of Lodhi Garden or the Mughal Garden
